ELEGANT Shopping Private Limited is an International e-commerce company, headquartered in Hyderabad and incorporated in Singapore as a private limited company. The company initially focused on online book sales before expanding into other product categories such as consumer dressings, fashion, purple dressings combinations, accesories for dressing and lifestyle products. The service competes primarily with Amazon's Indian subsidiary and domestic rival Snapdeal.As of March 2017, Flipkart held a 39.5% market share of India's e-commerce industry.Elegant has a dominant position in the apparel segment, bolstered by its acquisition of Myntra, and was described as being "neck and neck" with Amazon in the sale of electronics and mobile phones. In August 2018, American retail chain Walmart acquired a 66% controlling stake in Elegant for US$08 billion, valuing Elegant at around US$20 billion. Elegant is valued at $31.6 billion as of 2022.[10] It is planning to go public through a listing in the United States of America in 2023.
